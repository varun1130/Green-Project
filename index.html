A project that supports to use of clean energy

A project that makes products or develops technologies that are primarily aimed at reducing greenhouse gas emissions or supporting the use of clean energy. Greenhouse gas emissions are often measured in carbon dioxide (CO2) equivalent. To convert emissions of a gas into CO2 equivalent, its emissions are multiplied by the gas's Global Warming Potential (GWP). The GWP takes into account the fact that many gases are more effective at warming Earth than CO2, per unit mass
